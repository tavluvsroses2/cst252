Lab 4: Psuedocoding and Problem-Solving

By Tavia and Prisilla

Challenges

Task 1:Pseudocode an everyday task.

We did brushing your teeth for the first time.

  1. Get toothbrush and paste
  2. Wet toothbrush
  3. Brush the top and bottom right teeth in a circular motion
  4. Brush the top and bottom left teeth in a circular motion
  5. Rinse and spit

Task 2:Pseudocode a computer task.

We did Playing a memory game.

  1. Print out 6 random cards backs facing.
  2. Player chooses two cards.
  3. If match.
  4. Then match is a win.
  5. Give player points.
  6. Move cards to the side.
  7. Player chooses 2 cards.
  8. Else if no match
  9. Player repeats the process.
  10. No points given.
  11. Player chooses two cards
  12. If no cards are left. Print “YOU WIN!” screen.
